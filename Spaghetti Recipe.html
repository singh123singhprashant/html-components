Ingredients
  • Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water
    Instructions
  1. Bring water to a boil.
    2. Add spaghetti to boiling water.
  2. Add salt to taste.
      3. Note that
        "Add salt to taste." and "Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally." are in a sub-ordered list under the second instruction.
  3. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add marinara sauce to a pan, bringing to a simmer.
  5. Mix cooked spaghetti with marinara sauce.
  6. Enjoy!
